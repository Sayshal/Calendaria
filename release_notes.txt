## Bug Fixes

- Date-triggered notes fire within one tick of their scheduled time instead of waiting up to 30 minutes for the next check
- Event macros scheduled at midnight no longer race with auto-generated weather, so setWeather calls from date-triggered macros stick instead of getting overwritten
- Silent events now suppress only the chat announcement; their hooks and macros still run
- Toggling a note's visibility to Hidden no longer triggers Foundry v13 ownership validation errors
- Wind direction on setWeather accepts compass strings (N, NNE, NE, ...) or degrees, normalized to 0-360
